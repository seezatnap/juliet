# PRD: harry-potter-poem

## Goal
Write a poem about Harry Potter, delivering three distinct versions.

## Requirements
- Produce three separate poems about Harry Potter.
- Each version should be meaningfully different in tone or style.
- Keep the output suitable for a general audience.

## Non-Goals
- No analysis or explanation, only the poems.

## Deliverable
- Three labeled poem variants in the final response.
