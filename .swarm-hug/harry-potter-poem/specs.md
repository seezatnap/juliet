# PRD: Harry Potter Poems

## Goal
Create three distinct original poems about Harry Potter, reflecting the themes and world of the series.

## Requirements
- Provide 3 variations.
- Each poem should be original and avoid quoting copyrighted text.
- Focus on the Harry Potter world, characters, and themes.

## Deliverable
- Three labeled poems (Variation 1/2/3) in a single response.
