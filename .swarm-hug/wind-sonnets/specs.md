# PRD: wind-sonnets

## Goal
Write ten original sonnets about the wind.

## Requirements
- Produce 10 distinct sonnets.
- Each sonnet should be coherent, lyrical, and thematically focused on the wind.
- Keep formatting clear, with titles or numbering to distinguish each sonnet.

## Deliverable
A single output containing all ten sonnets.
